Taiya is an insular country guided by the people's belief in gods. The Empire, as the taiyanese call it, is governed by the Mastifs, a group of seven people that will fight in their gods' name. But the world is far from being perfect.

The World

In this world, you are a god and, because of greed, you decide to live in it. For that to happen, you have to share life with one of its citizens. Unfortunately, it didn't go as you intended.

You Can Change It All

Your decisions matter. The dialogue and the actions you take can change relations, perspectives and events. But be careful, since it can't always happen as you want.
